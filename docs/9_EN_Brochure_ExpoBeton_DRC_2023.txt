In order to accelerate integral development at the national and sub- regional levels, the Democratic Republic of Congo and its mining province of LUALABA, through the city of Kolwezi must be the locomotive of the establishment of a Coordination or Authority of the Southern Corridor between the Republic of Angola, the Republic of Zambia, the United Republic of Tanzania, the Republic of Namibia, the Republic of Botswana, Zimbabwe, Mozambique and South Africa. Facilitate the reliability of the corridor that extends from DRC/Kolwezi, Angola, Zambia, Namibia, Tanzania to Mozambique, with connectivity to Botswana and South Africa to return to KolweziExpoBeton RDC 2023 has chosen to place the issue of the Southern Corridor at the center of the reflections of the foundations of its 7th Edition 2.
